Frequently Asked Questions

What is VPickup?

VPickup is an online platform that allows customers to order items online and pick them up from nearby verified stores.

How does pickup work?

Place an order through VPickup, select a pickup slot, and collect your items from the store once the order is ready.

Are payments secure?

Yes. All payments are processed through secure and trusted payment gateways.

What if I face an issue with my order?

You can contact our Customer Care team through the support section for quick assistance.

Can I cancel an order?

Cancellation policies depend on the store and order status. Please refer to the order details or contact support.
