At VPickup, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our grocery delivery platform and services.

We believe in transparency and want you to feel confident about how we handle your information. This policy applies to all services offered by VPickup through our website, mobile applications, and any other platforms where this policy is posted.

Information We Collect

To provide you with exceptional service, we collect various types of information:

Personal Information

Name, email address, phone number, delivery address, and payment information necessary for order processing.

Order Information

Order history, preferences, special instructions, and any communication regarding your orders.

Technical Data

IP address, device information, browser type, operating system, and usage patterns through cookies and similar technologies.

Location Information

With your consent, we may collect precise or approximate location data to improve delivery accuracy and provide store recommendations.

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide, improve, and personalize our services:

Service Delivery

Processing orders, coordinating deliveries, communicating order status, and handling customer support.

Personalization

Tailoring product recommendations, remembering preferences, and creating a personalized shopping experience.

Service Improvement

Analyzing usage patterns to enhance platform functionality, optimize delivery routes, and develop new features.

Security & Compliance

Detecting and preventing fraudulent activities, ensuring platform security, and complying with legal obligations.

Data Protection & Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

Encryption

All sensitive data, including payment information, is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (SSL/TLS).

Access Controls

Strict access controls limit employee access to your personal information to only those who need it to perform their duties.

Regular Audits

We conduct regular security assessments and vulnerability testing to maintain the highest security standards.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

Third-Party Sharing

We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

Partner Stores

Necessary order details are shared with retail partners to fulfill your grocery orders.

Service Providers

Trusted third parties who assist in payment processing, delivery services, and technical support (under strict confidentiality agreements).

Legal Requirements

When required by law, court order, or governmental authority to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Important: We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Privacy Rights

You have control over your personal information:

Access & Correction

Request access to or correction of your personal information through your account settings or by contacting us.

Data Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and contractual restrictions.

Opt-Out Rights

Opt out of marketing communications at any time using the unsubscribe link in our emails or through account settings.

Data Portability

Request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format for transfer to another service.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

Essential Cookies

Required for basic platform functionality, such as maintaining your shopping cart and user session.

Analytical Cookies

Help us understand how users interact with our platform to improve performance and user experience.

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and preferences for a personalized experience.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

We're here to help you understand our privacy practices. Contact our Privacy Team with any questions or concerns.
